ReflexMe

Mental Health · Wellness Companion
Live MVP · Available Now
ReflexMe is a mental wellness app that helps people reflect, understand their emotions, keep a mood journal, breathe properly and analyze cognitive patterns. The user takes a psychological test during onboarding, gets a personal profile and uses a set of tools for daily self-work. The app includes psychology articles, exercises, affirmations, breathing techniques and a habit tracker — all in a minimalist, calm design.

✨ Key Features

  • Psychological onboarding test: 5 pattern categories, personal results & recommendations
  • 24/7 chat therapist: empathic sessions with crisis detection and multilingual support
  • Mood analytics: daily log with insights, trends and weekly/monthly charts
  • Reflection journal: structured diary for thoughts, feelings and observations
  • 4 breathing techniques: Box Breathing, 4-7-8, Coherent Breathing, Energizing
  • 90+ affirmations across categories for daily practice with notifications
  • Habit tracker: create, track & analyze with habit loop (trigger → action → reward)
  • Achievement system and gamification to keep users motivated

📚 Content & Capabilities

  • 40+ psychology articles: anxiety, self-esteem, comparison, productivity, burnout
  • 15+ exercises for mental health: meditations, visualizations, mindfulness practices
  • Available in 3 languages: English, Russian, Ukrainian. Expanding to 8+
  • Flexible subscription plans: Free, Premium and Ultra tiers
  • Smart reminders for practice, breathing and reflection sessions
  • Beautiful dark and light themes for comfortable use any time of day

NutriMe

Nutrition · Personal Dietitian
Planned · R&D
NutriMe is a mindful eating app. Users set goals, allergies and preferences — and receive personalized meal plans, recipes and daily calorie tracking. Includes food recognition via camera, automatic nutrient calculation and weekly reports.

🎯 Core Features

  • Personal onboarding: goals, weight, height, age, activity, allergies, preferences
  • Meal plan generation for day/week based on parameters and budget
  • Calorie counting: manual entry, barcode scanning, camera recognition
  • Macro (protein, fat, carbs) and micronutrient (vitamins, minerals) tracking
  • 500+ recipe library with diet filters (keto, vegan, paleo, etc.)
  • Shopping list — auto-generated from weekly meal plan

📊 Analytics & Features

  • Weekly reports: calorie trends, macros progress, recommendations
  • Water balance: hydration tracker with reminders throughout the day
  • Food diary: full meal history with any period overview
  • ReflexMe integration: emotional state and eating habits correlation
  • Social features: share recipes and progress with friends
  • Scheduled notifications for meals, snacks and water balance

FreeMe

Addiction Recovery · Recovery Support
Planned
FreeMe is for people who want to break free from addictions: smoking, alcohol, social media, sugar, caffeine. Combines a sobriety tracker, crisis support system, trigger mapping and anonymous community.

🛡️ Core Features

  • Sobriety tracker: days, hours, minutes without bad habit, visual milestones & rewards
  • Trigger mapping: identifying situations, emotions and places that provoke relapse
  • Crisis button: instant access to self-help techniques, breathing, motivation
  • Personal strategies: action plans for each trigger with step-by-step instructions
  • Progress journal: record feelings, victories, relapses — without judgment
  • Savings calculator: money and health saved by quitting the habit

👥 Community & Motivation

  • Anonymous community: support from people with the same experience
  • Achievement & badge system: gamified progress for motivation
  • Weekly and monthly reports: relapse patterns, improvements, trends
  • ReflexMe integration: emotional state and craving correlation
  • Motivational push notifications during "danger" hours
  • Multi-addiction support with separate trackers for each

DreamMe

Sleep · Recovery · Relaxation
Planned
DreamMe is a sleep quality improvement app. Poor sleep is the root of many problems: from anxiety to low productivity. DreamMe helps fall asleep faster, sleep deeper and wake up energized.

🌙 Core Features

  • Sleep tracking: automatic phase detection (light, deep, REM) via phone sensors
  • Smart alarm: wake up in optimal phase within set window
  • Sound library: white/pink/brown noise, nature, rain, ocean — 50+ tracks
  • Bedtime meditations: 10+ guided meditations for relaxation
  • 4-7-8 falling asleep technique: breathing with visual guide
  • Sleep stories: relaxing stories for adults as a sleep method

📈 Analytics & Integrations

  • Detailed analytics: duration, quality, time to fall asleep, awakenings
  • Weekly reports with trends and recommendations
  • Sleep score: personal sleep quality rating on a 100-point scale
  • Integration with Apple Health and Google Fit
  • Evening ritual: bedtime preparation checklist
  • ReflexMe integration: sleep and emotional state correlation
💪

FitMe

Fitness · Personal Trainer
Planned
FitMe is a personal fitness trainer in your pocket. Generates individual workout programs based on goals, fitness level and equipment.

🏋️ Core Features

  • Personalized programs: workouts for goals, level and equipment
  • 200+ exercises with video demos of proper technique
  • Workout timer: countdown, rest between sets, total time
  • Progressive overload: automatic difficulty increase
  • Warmup and stretching: built-in blocks before and after workouts
  • Workout calendar: weekly/monthly planning with reminders

🏆 Motivation & Analytics

  • Stats: volume load, workout count, working weights, progress
  • Social challenges: compete with friends
  • Streaks and badges for consistency
  • NutriMe integration: nutrition for workout type
  • DreamMe integration: sleep for heavy workouts
  • Body tracking: weight, measurements, progress photos
